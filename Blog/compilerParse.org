* Read X : α1α2 · · · αn, as
  “An X may be formed from the concatenation of an α1, α2, . . . ,
  αn .”
* Context free
  means can replace nonterminals in any order (i.e., re-
  gardless of context) to get same result (as long as you use same
  productions).
